About me

I’m a psychologist by training and a dataacrobat at heart. I have been working on my Ph.D. in cognitive neuroscience in Hamburg during the past 5 years. My everyday acrobatics evolve around data that stemms from behavioral and neuroscientific experiments designed to tackle how the brain processes spatial information for action. I’ll use this blog to broaden my view and answer questions that do not evolve around the brain. Thus, it documents the small and big steps in my play using non-experimental, freely available data, and useful things I have picked up along the way.

Beyond dataacrobatics in- and outside of academia, I’m crazy about my campervan, reading, biking, Yoga, longboarding, surfing, festivals, and (old school) Hip Hop.
